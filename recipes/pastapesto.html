Pasta Pesto Recipe

Description

This is one of the easiest pasta dishes to make.

With 20 minutes and a jar of pesto, you can create a dish that will truly impress.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of any kind
  • Pesto of any kind
  • Diced chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Good vibes

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Fry chicken in olive oil
  3. Mix pasta and chicken
  4. Mix pesto into pasta and chicken
  5. Enjoy!
